Bibo, an alien from the distant planet Creatia, came to study abroad on a mysterious and colorful planet called Earth because of his deep curiosity. Among the countless stars, this planet, full of emotions, colors, and creativity, was a place that gave Bibo a special feeling and inspiration.

Here, Bibo learns design, UX, and digital technology, and learns how to communicate with people on Earth through design. In the process, he realizes the power of design to move people's hearts, not just acquires simple skills, and continues to take on new challenges every day with joy and passion as his driving force.

This portfolio is a record of such a study abroad experience on Earth. It provides a fresh perspective on creativity and cultural values ​​from a unique perspective as a person from another planet.
